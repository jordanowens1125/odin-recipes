Fettucine Recipe

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Lasagna first made is a grand entrance in Naples, Italy during the Middle Ages in the 14th century. Initially, this dish was reserved for special events and holidays.

Ingrediants

  • 1 pound fettuccine
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of half and half
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Chopped fresh parsley, optional

Steps

  1. Prepare fettuccine according to instructions on box.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Add half & half, reduce heat to simmer and cook for five minutes.
  4. Add Parmesan cheese, nutmeg and pepper and cook for another minute until sauce is smooth.
